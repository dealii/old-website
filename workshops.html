The deal.II Workshops

To foster interaction within the community of deal.II users and developers, we regularly run workshops on deal.II and its development.

In these workshops, users are encouraged to present their work based on deal.II, and developers present new features and future directions of development. Usually, these workshops feature a casual environment conducive to easy interaction and productivity. This typically includes "hackathon" sessions during which there are no formal talks and people work on individual or joint projects.

Since the inception of deal.II, workshops were organized as follows:

  • Heidelberg, Germany 2006
  • Heidelberg, Germany 2010
  • Heidelberg, Germany 2012
  • College Station, TX, USA 2013
  • Heidelberg, Germany 2014 (as part of PDESoft 2014)
