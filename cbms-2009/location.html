Location Information for the 2009 CBMS Conference on AFEM

The conference lectures will take place in Room 117 on the first floor of the Blocker Building at Texas A&M University. This is the diagonal building on Ireland Street, just south of University Drive. See the green arrow on the map below. It is behind the construction site of the almost completed physics building (though the latter is not yet visible on the satellite image below).

If you are driving directly to the conference site, you can either park at St. Mary's parking lot or in the North Side Garage (blue arrows).

The Hampton Inn is at 320 Texas Ave. South (red arrow), in walking distance of the campus.
