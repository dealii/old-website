Conference Topics

  • Use cases and applications of the library

  • What users think would be useful directions for the library to go into, things that are missing, and possibly getting people together who can help implement those parts

  • Newer parts of the library (e.g. massively parallel computing, multiphysics coupling, etc) and how these could help in your programs.

Travel Information to Heidelberg

  • By Airport Shuttle (from Frankfurt Airport)
    Lufthansa offers a daily shuttle service between Frankfurt airport and the center of Heidelberg. For further information please follow this link.
  • By Airport Shuttle (from Frankfurt Hahn Airport or Baden Airport)
    Hahn Express offers a daily shuttle service both between Frankfurt Hahn airport and Heidelberg and between Baden airport and Heidelberg. For further information please follow this link.
  • By Train
    Heidelberg is well connected to the rail network of the Deutsche Bahn (DB). For further information and travel planning please follow this link.
  • By Car
    Heidelberg is best reached via the Bundesautobahn A5/E35. Take the Heidelberg exit number 37 at the Heidelberg Interchange. Then merge onto A656/B37 towards Heidelberg. For travel planning to and within Heidelberg you can use e.g. Google Maps.
  • Local transportation
    For travel planning for all means of local transportation (buses, tramway) please follow this link (only German) or this link (some information in English). The best tramway stops are "Technologiepark" and "Bunsengymnasium", both stops are part of the tramway lines 21 and 24. The best bus stop is "Pädagogische Hochschule" which is part of bus line 31.
