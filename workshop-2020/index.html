Eighth deal.II Users and Developers Workshop

Virtual one-day meeting, May 26, 2020

Program and Topics

We will be hosting an online only one-day workshop on recent developments of the deal.II library.
The workshop will be available live using Zoom and will be recorded and available after the fact. The focus is on recent developments and the new 9.2.0 release.
