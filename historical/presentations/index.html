Presentations on and with deal.II

On this page, we collect a few presentations that have been given on the development of deal.II as well as the use of the library for applications. Some of them are quite nice and colorful!

We would be happy to host other presentations here as well. If you have one that you gave on an application of deal.II, drop us a note and a pdf file.

Some other resources on the use of deal.II or its development can also be found on a page where publications are collected.

Presentations at the deal.II User Workshop in Heidelberg, August 23-25, 2010

This is a subset of the presentations given at the 2010 deal.II user's meeting in Heidelberg:
  • Wolfgang Bangerth: A history of deal.II and the way forward
  • Wolfgang Bangerth: Using multicore machines with deal.II
  • Bärbel Janssen: Multilevel methods with local smoothing
  • Martin Kronbichler: Generic and efficient solvers for coupled flow/transport problems
  • Andrew McBride: Coupled problems in nonlinear solid mechanics: non-Fickian diffusion
  • Martin Weiler: Nonlinear biharmonic equation and very weak Laplace problem
